Jason Hand

Senior Cloud Ops Advocate @ Microsoft

Jason Hand is a Senior Cloud Advocate at Microsoft, focusing on the Azure Cloud platform. Frequent speaker and author on the subjects of site reliability engineering, post-incident reviews, and chatops, Jason enjoys connecting with technologists around the world on ideas related to building resilient systems.

Co-organizer and emcee of the annual DevOpsDays Rockies conference, the Frontrange Site Reliability Meetup, Denver DevOps Meetup, and DevOps Road Trip, Jason enjoys connecting story tellers and actionable ideas with those who are hungry to learn.

Co-host of the podcast “Community Pulse”, Jason loves bringing together ideas and expertise as it relates to building community within tech (I.e. advocacy, evangelism).
