@jaydestro (Jay Gordon) during this year's Microsoft Ignite | The Tour

May 21, 2019 Â· 1 minute read

Jay and I joined Microsoft around the same time and for a brief moment were even on the same team within the larger Cloud Advocacy team.

Every time we got to speak at the same stop on the Microsoft Ignite | The Tour, we tended to gravitate towards each other and despite vastly different tastes in music, we have a lot in common and it’s always good to catch up.

I recently had the pleasure of being a guest on Jay’s show. When you get a second, check out his show “On-call Horror Stories”.

Much of Jay’s background is rooted in the database and system’s administrator world. If you’re curious on how to move older apps using older database technology, check out his talk on “Migrating web applications to Azure”. It’s a solvable problem but there’s a lot to consider once you’ve realized it’s time for the move.
