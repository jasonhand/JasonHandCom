Erik St. Martin - Microsoft Ignite | The Tour

May 21, 2019 Â· 1 minute read

Hands-down… the most amazing experience of this year’s “Microsoft Ignite | The Tour” was riding along with Erik in a Ferrari 488 Spider through not only the streets of Milan but along Lake Como and up the mountain to the observatory.

Check out this video I made of the experience.

You’d be hard pressed to find anyone who knows more about Kubernetes than Erik and that’s exactly what he was in Amsterdam to talk about when we got another chance to hang out.

If you are new to containers or even an “expert” in Kubernetes, you should watch both of these talks from Erik. I bet there’s a lot you didn’t know. There certainly was for me.
