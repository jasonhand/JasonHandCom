Microsoft's Startup Tour 2019

April 5, 2019 Â· 2 minute read

New York City & Toronto

It’s been about 8 months since joining the Azure Advocacy Team at Microsoft and I can hardly believe how fast it’s gone by.

Being part of the global event “Microsoft Ignite | The Tour” has been an incredible experience. I’ve met so many amazing people all over the world who are as excited about learning from us as we are about advocating for them.

With the tour winding down, I’ve been focusing a bit more of my advocacy efforts on helping startups through various phases of their journey.

I’ve always enjoyed the startup experience and can’t wait to work with entrepreneurs all over the world through various phases and challenges of building a tech startup.

On Tuesday, April 9th I’ll be joining several others from the advocacy team in New York City for the first of a series of FREE, one-day events aimed at supporting growing startups.

Throughout the day, attendees will hear from a number of advocates and experts on subjects such as:

  • Setting Goals and Objectives
  • Scaling Teams
  • Creating Secure Systems
  • Mobile Applications
  • Containers
  • Kubernets
  • Serverless
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Big Data
  • Governance

I’ll be talking about “Building a Minimum Viable Incident Response Plan”.

Currently there is a waiting list for New York, but a few spots are still available in Toronto.

If you’re interested in attending this FREE event, you can register or join the waiting list here.

I can’t wait to meet everyone. These are going to be really fun events.

See you soon.

j.
